Recipe for Fried Rice

Ingredients required

  • Boiled rice
  • vegetables of all type
  • spices as per taste
  • sauses(green chilli,tomato sauce...)
  • oil

Steps to prepare Fried Rice

  1. Take oil in the utensil,heat it.
  2. After the oil is heated, put all the sliced vegetables in the utensil and stir it well and let the vegetables boil
    3. Put all the spices and stir to mix all the vegetables and spices
  3. Put the sauses of choice(for taste) and stir
  4. Once everything is set put the boiled rice in it and stir well
  5. Close the lid for sometime
  6. Fried rice is ready to be served
